Risks and mitigation
Security, compliance, and delivery risk register
Security and compliance
Data sensitivity
Prompts themselves contain no PII. Users must not paste individual performance data or salary figures into external AI tools. Acceptable use policy required.
Copilot data residency
Microsoft Copilot for M365 uses tenant-scoped data with Microsoft's enterprise data protection. No training on customer data. Confirm tenant configuration with IT.
External AI tools
For Claude / ChatGPT fallback: use enterprise accounts where available. Update acceptable use policy accordingly.
SharePoint permissions
Prompt library is internal only. No anonymous access. All access via corporate identity (Entra ID).
AI output accuracy
All AI output is clearly labelled as a draft requiring human review. No AI output goes directly to Workday without leader approval in MVP phase.
Risk register
High
Low adoption — leaders revert to HR
Mitigation: Executive sponsor mandate. HR actively directs all new objective requests to the tool first. HR tracks adoption and escalates to CPO.
High
AI output quality below expectation
Mitigation: Pilot prompts with 10 executives first. Iterate before broad rollout. Governance committee reviews quality monthly.
Medium
Users paste sensitive data into external AI
Mitigation: Acceptable use policy. Training on what is and isn't appropriate to share. Use placeholders in prompts that guide safe inputs.
Medium
Prompt library becomes stale / outdated
Mitigation: Annual mandatory review in governance calendar. Strategy-pivot triggers ad hoc review. Version status visible on every prompt.
Medium
Copilot license gaps across org
Mitigation: Free fallback options documented and tested. Power Automate flow for non-licensed users in Phase 3.
Low
SharePoint governance overhead
Mitigation: Keep governance lightweight in MVP. Power Automate handles approvals automatically. Committee meets monthly not weekly.
Low
Workday AI launches sooner than expected
Mitigation: Prompt library is an asset not a liability. Prompts, governance model and feedback data all transfer directly to Workday AI onboarding.
