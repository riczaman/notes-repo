Pipeline (Vendor Expense) intake

The strategic go-ahead approval, required for all 3rd-party spend over $100K and submitted at least 90 days before the procurement engagement. Once approved, you'll continue straight into the USFF sourcing intake from My Requests — no re-entering the triage.

⚠ This is a separate approval cycle, worked through BOM agendas. When every level has approved, this request flips to “Ready to submit in USFF” in My Requests and a button takes you into the sourcing intake with your answers pre-populated.

SPID — Vendor Expense (Signing Party)

The final step, after everything else is complete: pipeline approved → USFF sourcing intake approved → Ariba SR number in hand. SPID validates the request against what was already approved and only asks the net-new questions the USFF intake didn't capture — no re-typing what the system already knows.

ⓘ Prerequisites checked automatically: a fully-approved Pipeline number AND your approved USFF intake number (or its Ariba SR number — both carry the same record). Anything that changed since the pipeline was approved is flagged in red so approvers see the discrepancy immediately.
