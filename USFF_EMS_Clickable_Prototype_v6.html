Approval agendas — BOM view

Agendas are built from pipeline STATUS: a request at "VP Pending" belongs on a VP agenda. The BOM Lead builds the agenda here, the Excel is emailed to attendees automatically, outcomes are recorded per item (with comments & attachments), and each approval advances the pipeline to its next required level. Skipped items auto-requeue to the TOP of the next agenda with their original scheduled date kept for audit (rules 22–24).

ⓘ How this maps to their Pipeline Approval Portal App: their PowerApp splits Generate Agenda / Gallery / Modify / Close across separate pages, with the agenda Excel hyperlinked in an email. We kept all four capabilities but scoped them to ONE drill-down context (Segment → Level → BU → Date) — you always know which meeting you're acting on, and outcomes live on the same record as the approvals. Still owed by the business: the master SharePoint skills list, the rate-table repository pointer, and the full expense policy document.
